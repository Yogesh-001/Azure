What We Offer?

About Earth

We believe that the earth is what sustains and keeps us. It is a truth we understand deeply and we let it inspire and influence every aspect of our operations. Thank you.

We have also come to realise that working with the earth is a two way project. We need to give back as much, perhaps more, than we take from her to keep things in balance and working well. Eco systems and local environments are delicate and interconnected.

What We Do?

Our Motive

The Earth Project believes in the Native American Spirit that “We do not inherit the land from our ancestors but borrow it from our children”. Because Our earth is a precious gift, we need to honor and protect her.

Saving our earth and its environment becomes highly important as it provide us food and water to sustain life. Our well-being solely depends on this planet it gives food and water to all living things to it is our responsibility to take care of it.

Who We Are?

Team

We are a community of educators who believe in the power of education to change the world. We see that impact results from small, connected actions in spaces where learning is connected, co-constructed, and accessible.

We Must Act Now. Before we’ve lost irreplaceable species and ecosystems forever.
